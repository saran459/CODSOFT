Key Features

Feature 1

Another new feature in the Thar family that debuted in the Thar Roxx is a 10.25-inch digital instruments cluster.

Feature 2

While the three-door Thar is only offered with rear parking sensors, the Thar Roxx gets a far more modern 360-degree surround view system and blind view monitor although, only in the top-spec AX7L trim. All other variants except for the base trim get a rear camera.

Feature 3

The Thar Roxx is offered with a raft of safety features, with level-2 ADAS being one of them. This includes functions such as forward collision warning (FCW), auto emergency braking (AEB), adaptive cruise control (ACC), smart pilot assist (SPA), lane departure warning (LDW), lane keep assist (LKA), and high beam assist (HBA) among others.
